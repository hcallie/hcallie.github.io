ZOMBIE CAKES

Bake pastries, shoot zombies, and escape the apocalypse in this co-op shooter wave survival game (XBox controllers needed). In this game, you and a partner interact with machines to create different tasty pastries inside of your foodtruck. These pastries are then loaded into a turret and shot out at the approaching waves of zombies. Collect coins to buy more recipes, gadgets, and reinforcements to build your foodtruck into a gluten shootin' powerhouse. Featuring cartoony art, funny characters, and a rollercoaster storyline, this game is a great experince and was really fun to make. It also won the EECS 494 F22 Showcase!

Zelda

I recreated a dungeon level in The Legend of Zelda for NES with my partner, Alexandra Lavacek. The game featured the Stalfos, Goriya, Gel, and Keese enemies from the original game, and mirrored the layout original Legend of Zelda as closely as possible. Additionally, we implemented our own custom mechanic, a teleportation wand, which allows players to switch the location of two objects (or of another object and themselves). With this custom mechanic came a set of custom levels, which can be reached by pressing "4" on the keyboard.
